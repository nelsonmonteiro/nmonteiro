What is a micro app?

A micro app is a collection of tasks defined by the user triggered by and event, time or data change on devices. In other words, it's a list of "when -> do" events understandable to any user, even without any programming expertises. Since micro apps are running in a cloud, they can listen and run tasks on multiple devices at the same time.

Create your first cloud app

How does it work?

1

Connect your devices

Connect your devices to your DJINA account. Share them with your friends or make them public to allow others to create apps with them.

2

Create your tasks

Enumerate the actions you want to do when some conditions on your devices are met. Examples: When my car is less then 5 km from home, turn on the heater. When the mobile of my child is father than 2 km from home, send me and him a notification.

3

Is ready to be used

After defining your tasks, the micro app is immediately ready to be used without any installation.
